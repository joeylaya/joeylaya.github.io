WHAT WE DO

At Twalo, we work with educational professionals to advocate for—and actualize—liberatory environments for youth and students.

Together, we navigate how to embrace young people as their whole selves by addressing the root issues of inequity and oppression.

Why Twalo?

Liberation-minded

Our work is guided by a core objective: liberation for everyone. This manifests in the content that we create and our approach to consulting as a whole.

Action-oriented

We prioritize building capacity for making actionable change--all backed by research, theory, and evidence-based best practices.

Community-centered

As a Black, Asian, trans, and queer owned organization, our work is informed not only by our professional and educational experiences, but by our personal experiences too.

We are a part of the communities that we talk about, so we bring authenticity and insight to our work.
